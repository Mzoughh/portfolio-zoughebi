<h4 id="VR_Exemples" style="margin-top: 70px;">VR Exemples</h4>

<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;"><strong>Enhanced Learning Assistant with Augmented Reality</strong>: This idea involves creating an augmented reality application that functions as a learning assistant. Users would wear AR glasses and could interact with virtual information that overlays the real world. For example, when learning new skills, users could see instructions and tips floating in front of them, simplifying the learning process.</p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;"><strong>Augmented Reality for Medical Assistance</strong>: This idea focuses on the use of augmented reality to assist healthcare professionals. Doctors could use AR devices to obtain real-time information about patients, visualize crucial medical data directly in their field of vision, and even collaborate remotely with other experts to diagnose complex cases.</p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;"><strong>AR for Repair and Maintenance</strong>: In this concept, augmented reality would be used to enhance the skills of maintenance technicians. By wearing AR glasses, technicians could access repair diagrams and instructions directly in front of them, simplifying the maintenance process and reducing equipment downtime.</p>
</div>

<h4 id="Prototype" style="margin-top: 70px;">Prototype</h4>


<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>AR Language Enhancer:</strong><br>
        It is a wearable device in the form of smart glasses with augmented reality features. The goal of this device is to enhance intercultural communication in real-time.
    </p>
    <ul style="list-style-type: none;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
            <strong>Features:</strong>
            <ul style="list-style-type: disc; margin-left: 20px;">
                <li>Real-Time Translation: When a person wears these glasses and interacts with someone speaking a foreign language, the device uses voice recognition and automatic translation to display subtitles in the user's language directly in their field of vision.</li>
                <li>Advanced Facial Recognition: The glasses are equipped with facial recognition software that identifies the person you are talking to and displays relevant information about them, such as their name, title, company, etc.</li>
                <li>Contextualization: The device also analyzes the context of the conversation, such as location, time, and nearby events, to provide relevant contextual information, enabling smoother and more meaningful communication.</li>
                <li>Display of Relevant Information: The glasses can display real-time information, such as weather updates, restaurant recommendations, translations of signs, etc., directly in the user's field of vision.</li>
                <li>Gesture Interaction: The user can interact with the interface using simple gestures, making it intuitive to use.</li>
                <li>Customization: The device allows the user to customize the appearance and style of subtitles, as well as how information is displayed on the screen.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
    </ul>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        This prototype aims to enhance communication skills and the experience of people traveling in foreign countries or interacting with individuals from different cultures. It aligns with the theme of enhancing human abilities in the age of augmented reality by making intercultural communication more fluid and accessible. You could draw a diagram of these smart glasses in action, with subtitles and contextual information displayed in the user's field of vision.
    </p>
</div>

<h4 id="SShape" style="margin-top: 70px;">Evolution of smartphone shape</h4>

<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        The evolution of smartphone design over the years has been intriguing. Early mobile phones were relatively simple, primarily rectangular in shape, featuring physical keyboards. However, with technological advancements and progress in industrial design, smartphones underwent a significant transformation. Here is a discussion on their evolution, the reasons behind these changes, and what the future might hold:
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        Shift from Physical Keyboards to Touchscreens:
        Initially, mobile phones came equipped with physical keyboards for text input and numerical entry. One of the key drivers of this change was the introduction of touchscreens, which offered several advantages in terms of accessibility and usability.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        Advantages of Touchscreens:
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li style="text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">Versatility: Touchscreens allow adaptable user interfaces, featuring virtual keyboards, buttons, and gestures as needed.</li>
        <li style="text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">Ease of Use: Touch interactions are natural and intuitive for most users, reducing accessibility barriers.</li>
        <li style="text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">Customization: Users can personalize their interfaces, adjusting text size, colors, and more to their preferences.</li>
        <li style="text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">Scalability: Software updates can introduce new features without requiring hardware modifications.</li>
    </ul>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        Current and Future Shape Evolution:
        Presently, smartphones are trending toward slimmer, lighter, and larger designs. However, a major transformation currently underway is the emergence of foldable smartphones. These devices combine the advantages of large screens for content consumption with a more compact form for portability. Foldable smartphones offer a glimpse into what the future of mobile devices might entail.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        Slimness and Lightness: Future smartphones could become even thinner, lighter, and more flexible. Imagine devices resembling cards that are easy to carry.
    </p>
</div>

<h4 id="Computer" style="margin-top: 70px;">The Computer of the 21st Century</h4>

<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>Ubiquitous Computing Progress:</strong><br>
        We are edging closer to a world of ubiquitous computing, thanks to the widespread use of smartphones, the growing network of IoT devices, and the emergence of voice assistants. While these technologies have made computing more integrated into our daily lives, we have not yet achieved the complete invisibility and seamlessness envisioned by Mark Weiser. Manual interactions with computers are still commonplace in various aspects of our lives.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>Key Advancements:</strong><br>
        Several critical advancements have paved the way for the realization of ubiquitous computing. The widespread availability of high-speed internet and wireless networks has been fundamental, enabling devices to communicate seamlessly. Advances in hardware miniaturization have made it possible to create smaller, more portable computing devices, aligning with the vision of computers that are unobtrusive and blend into our surroundings. Furthermore, the development of AI and machine learning algorithms allows computers to better understand and respond to user needs, an essential aspect of creating responsive and context-aware computing environments.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>What's Needed for the Vision:</strong><br>
        To get closer to the vision of ubiquitous computing, certain key factors need to be addressed. Increased interoperability between different devices and systems is essential for a seamless and integrated computing experience. Additionally, we must establish more robust privacy and security measures to protect user data and personal information, especially as more aspects of our lives become digitally connected. Focusing on improving the user experience is vital; computing should be unobtrusive, intuitive, and assistive, with devices that understand user context and adapt accordingly. Lastly, as we create more connected devices, we must consider their environmental impact and work towards sustainable computing solutions.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>Conclusion:</strong><br>
        While significant progress has been made in the direction of ubiquitous computing, we have not fully realized the vision yet. Achieving true ubiquitous computing will require further advancements in interoperability, user experience, privacy, security, and sustainability, with the goal of creating computing environments that are seamlessly integrated into our daily lives.
    </p>
</div>

<h4 id="MR" style="margin-top: 70px;">Mixed Reality</h4>


<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        In the near future, where Mixed Reality Head-Mounted Displays (MR HMDs) have become popular, we present two innovative application concepts that leverage the potential of this technology to enhance different aspects of our lives.
    </p>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>Virtual History Guide</strong><br>
        <em>Concept:</em><br>
        The "Virtual History Guide" application is designed for history enthusiasts, students, and tourists eager to learn more about historical sites. This application uses MR technology to offer interactive tours of historical sites, museums, and monuments from around the world.
    </p>
    <ul style="text-indent: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <li><strong>Immersive Tours:</strong> Users can explore historical sites, view 3D reconstructions of ancient buildings and historical artifacts, and interact with virtual historical figures.</li>
        <li><strong>Educational Narration:</strong> Virtual guides provide detailed historical information, anecdotes, and narratives about past events.</li>
        <li><strong>Customization:</strong> Users can select the time period, location, and topic that interests them the most to personalize their learning experience.</li>
        <li><strong>Social Sharing:</strong> Users can share their discoveries with fellow history enthusiasts through social media and online communities.</li>
    </ul>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        This application allows users to relive history in an immersive and educational way while visiting historical sites from around the world without leaving their homes.
    </p>
</div>

<div style="flex: 1;">
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <strong>HoloShop</strong><br>
        <em>Concept:</em><br>
        "HoloShop" is a groundbreaking mixed reality application that reinvents the online shopping experience. It enables users to virtually browse 3D stores, try on clothing and products, and make online purchases with an unprecedented level of interaction and personalization.
    </p>
    <ul style="text-indent: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        <li><strong>Personalized Virtual Stores:</strong> Users can create their own virtual avatars, dress them as they please, and visit virtual fashion, electronics, furniture, and other stores.</li>
        <li><strong>Virtual Try-On:</strong> Users can try on clothing, glasses, jewelry, and even move virtual furniture in a mixed reality environment to see how they fit in their home.</li>
        <li><strong>Live Reviews:</strong> Friends and family can join the user in mixed reality to provide feedback on purchases, making the shopping experience more social.</li>
        <li><strong>Interactive Purchases:</strong> Users can make purchases directly from the application, selecting their choice in 3D and adding items to their cart.</li>
    </ul>
    <p style="text-indent: 20px; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px;">
        "HoloShop" aims to reinvent online shopping by creating a more interactive, realistic, and fun shopping experience through mixed reality.
    </p>
</div>
